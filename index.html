It can be hard to find people to study with when taking a class. Unless you are an extrovert, talking to strangers and forming a study group isn't a smooth process for everyone. Studymates is a platform where you can find people from your class and make plans to study together! All you have to do is send us the screenshot of your class schedule and we'll take care of the rest :)
